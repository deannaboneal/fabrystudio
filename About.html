HISTORY

Fabry Studio has been in business for over 50 years in Nashville, Tennessee. We are specialists in the photographic print industry helping professional performing and recording artists get their faces out there! Let us help you put your best face forward. Fabry Studio has the experience you need to get your project handled regardless of the format. We handle digital files exactly as you send them or we can edit, alter and enhance the look you want to portray to the public. We can also scan your publicity photos or art work and have the ability to add captions, text and just about anything else you desire. We also handle restoring old lithographs or prints and can ensure you will be happy with the results.

Fabry Studio of Nashville, Tennessee has been in business for over 50 years. Our clients number among the biggest and best known in the publishing and recording industry. You generally won't find us in advertisements or other publications. Our customers come back for the quality and reliability they can count on over and over again.

Fabry Studio has stood the test of time while migrating into the digital world as well as maintaining the traditional photographic retouching techniques. We're located in the heart of downtown Nashville, Tennessee on 8th Avenue South. Just a short distance from Music Row and other prominent Nashville landmarks. However, you don't have to live in Nashville to get Nashville's premiere promotional photo/imaging/reproduction service, we cater to clients worldwide.
